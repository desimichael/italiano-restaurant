About Us

All of our menu items are inspired by Italian cuisine with a bit of French accent. These creations have been created by our head chef, Reberto, after studying authentic Italian cuisine in Italy. Not only do we have fresh flown-in seafood from the northeast, but we also have a variety of handcrafted cocktails, wine, and beer to choose from. Come dine with us & experience an authentic Italian meal in an intimate dining space. We look forward to serving you really soon!
